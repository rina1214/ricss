01
ORIGIN: VIGAN CITY & SANTA

Ilocos Empanada

The iconic orange crispy crust filled with savory ingredients! A must-try street food.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Rice flour & 1 cup All-purpose flour
  • 1 cup Annatto water (for orange color)
  • Grated green papaya or bean sprouts
  • Longganisa or ground pork
  • Hard-boiled eggs (sliced)
  • Oil for frying

Steps to Cook

Step 1: Make the Dough

Mix the flours and add annatto water gradually. Mix until it becomes a smooth, orange dough. Set aside.

Step 2: Prepare Filling

Sauté the papaya/sprouts and meat separately with garlic, onion, and fish sauce until cooked.

Step 3: Mold & Fry

Flatten a portion of dough into a thin circle. Put fillings and egg in the center. Fold and seal edges. Deep fry until golden brown and crispy!

02
ORIGIN: LAPOG & CANDON

Crispy Bagnet

The ultimate pork delicacy! Super crispy skin and tender meat inside.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Pork Belly (Liempo)
  • Water, Salt, Pepper, Garlic
  • Vinegar
  • Lots of Oil for deep frying

Steps to Cook

Step 1: Boil

Boil the pork belly with salt, pepper, and garlic until tender. Drain and let it dry completely.

Step 2: Prick & Air Dry

Prick the skin with a fork. Rub with vinegar and salt. Air dry or put in front of electric fan for hours.

Step 3: Deep Fry

Fry in very hot oil. Low heat then high heat method. Fry until super crispy and bubbly. Slice and serve with bagoong!

03
ORIGIN: THROUGHOUT ILOCOS SUR

Pinakbet

Healthy mixed vegetables stewed in shrimp paste.

Ingredients

  • Squash, Ampalaya, Okra, Eggplant, Beans
  • Pork belly or shrimp
  • Onion, Garlic, Tomatoes
  • Bagoong Alamang (Shrimp paste)
  • Water

Steps to Cook

Step 1: Sauté

Heat oil. Sauté garlic, onion, and tomatoes. Add the pork and cook until light brown.

Step 2: Season

Add the bagoong alamang. Pour water and let it simmer for a few minutes.

Step 3: Add Vegetables

Add the squash first, then the other vegetables. Do not over stir. Cover and cook until tender but not mushy.

04
ORIGIN: VIGAN CITY

Vigan Longganisa

Garlicky and savory sausage known for its distinct taste.

Ingredients

  • Ground Pork
  • Lots of minced Garlic
  • Soy sauce, Vinegar, Salt, Sugar, Black Pepper
  • Annatto powder (optional for color)
  • Sausage casing

Steps to Make

Step 1: Mix

Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Mix thoroughly until well blended.

Step 2: Stuff

Place the mixture into the sausage casing using a funnel or sausage stuffer.

Step 3: Cook

Pan-fry with a little water until water dries up, then add oil and fry until golden brown.

05
ORIGIN: POPULAR DESSERT EVERYWHERE

Halo-Halo

The ultimate Filipino cold dessert! A perfect mixture of sweet ingredients, ice, and milk.

Ingredients

  • Finely crushed ice
  • Evaporated milk or Condensed milk
  • Sweetened beans, sago, gulaman, nata de coco
  • Sweetened banana, jackfruit, macapuno
  • Ube halaya, Leche flan, Ice cream

Steps to Make

Step 1: Layer

Put the sweet ingredients (beans, jellies, fruits) at the bottom of a tall glass.

Step 2: Add Ice

Fill the glass to the top with finely crushed ice. Press it down gently.

Step 3: Top & Mix

Add the leche flan, ube halaya, and ice cream on top. Pour milk. Then... HALO-HALO! Mix everything and enjoy! 😋
